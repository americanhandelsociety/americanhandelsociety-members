Members of the American Handel Society can also become: a member of the Georg-Friedrich-Händel-Gesellschaft in Halle (to receive the Händel-Jahrbuch) or a Friend of The Handel Institute in Great Britain (to receive The Handel Institute Newsletter).

Members of Georg-Friedrich-Händel-Gesellschaft can join the Goettingen and Karlsruhe Handel societies for half price. The below provides three options for bundling together memberships.

Single membership – Georg-Friedrich-Händel-Gesellschaft*

Dual membership – Georg-Friedrich-Händel-Gesellschaft / Göttinger-Händel-Gesellschaft*†

Dual membership - Georg-Friedrich-Händel-Gesellschaft / Handel-Gesellschaft Karlsruhe*

Triple membership – Georg-Friedrich-Händel-Gesellschaft / Göttinger-Händel-Gesellschaft / Händel-Gesellschaft Karlsruhe*

* This organization does not have a reduced rate for retirees.
† This organization has additional categories of Regular Membership that require a higher membership fee but provide additional benefits (see its website). Arrangements for these other categories may be made directly with Mrs. Pomeroy Kelly by mail: Marjorie Pomeroy Kelly, Secretary/Treasurer, THE AMERICAN HANDEL SOCIETY, 49 Christopher Hollow Road, Sandwich, MA 02563.
