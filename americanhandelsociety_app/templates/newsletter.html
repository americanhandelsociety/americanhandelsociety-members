Newsletter

The American Handel Society publishes a newsletter three times a year (Spring, Summer, Winter); it contains reports on scholarly developments, notices of upcoming conferences and performances, reviews, lists of recent publications, and news of the AHS.

Minji Kim, editor of the newsletter, welcomes comments and suggestions for future issues. Please email minjik@gmail.com.

Newsletter Archives

Newsletters are available in PDF format. Members of the AHS can view all Newsletters, including the latest three issues; non-members can view previews of the latest three issues. Complete Newsletters become publicly available online one year after their publication.
